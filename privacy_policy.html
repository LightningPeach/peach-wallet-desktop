Information that you provide at the time of registering for use of, or access to, the Software. b. Transaction information (sender, amount, receiver) is transmitted from your wallet to the server when a streaming payment or a payment through Lightning_id (public key) is initiated, but it is not stored. c. Your IP address, which we automatically receive and record when you interact with our Software is only kept for the duration of one session while your wallet is online. d. Lightning_id / Public key of your wallet (unique identifier of lightning node in lightning network in hex representation), is only kept for the duration of one session while your wallet is online. By clicking on the optional consent button, you allow collection of anonymized usage statistics via Google Analytics and Firebase. We do NOT collect, nor have access to the following information: a. Your private keys from cryptocurrencies; these are only accessible through your own device; and b. information on the transactions you perform through the use of the Software. 2. How we collect your data Most of the personal data we process is information that you knowingly provide to us directly, while using the Software. 3. The purposes for which your data are used We use information that we collect about you or that you provide to us, including any personal data for the following main purposes: a. To provide our services When you provide the IP address and Public Key, we process them to route streaming payments, and payments via Public Key that are initiated by the sender without a corresponding recipient payment request. Transaction information is only used to route messages between wallet users if there is no payment request from the recipient. If the recipient does send a payment request, the server does not participate in this exchange at all and the information is not shared. b. For statistical research General. Subject to your consent, we use automatic tools to perform statistical research into general trends regarding the use of our services. Such anonymized information may include: • Device type • Button clicks • Screen openings • Session duration • Geographic location Interests and benefits. Statistical research helps us to develop better services and offerings, to improve the design and content of our Software. c.
