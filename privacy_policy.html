Changes to Privacy Policy 10. Contacting Us 1. What information do we collect? There are two privacy modes available in the Software: Extended and Standard. In the Software, we provide a switch toggle which allows a user to switch between the two modes. The Extended mode introduces additional features but requires minimal interaction with Peach servers to work. Standard mode excludes any communication with external servers at the expense of the following three features being disabled: Recurring/Stream payments, Payments without a user-generated Lightning invoice, and Contacts. The privacy mode can be changed anytime within the Profile screen of the Wallet. If Standard mode is activated, we do not collect or process any personal information about the wallet and its usage. In other words, the Software does not connect or interact with any Peach servers at any moment in this mode. A privacy mode choice is offered to a user during the first run of the Wallet and is made available as a setting in the Software. If the Extended mode is activated, we process the following personal data: (a) Transaction information (sender’s and receiver’s public keys) is transmitted from your wallet to the Peach server when a recurring/stream payment or a payment through Lightning_id (public key) is initiated (invoice less payments). This data is never stored, and the Peach server only routes this data to the payment recipient. The transaction is end-to-end encrypted on the wallet side and the Peach server does not have access to any data except from where from and where to the payment needs to be routed. (b) Your IP address, which we automatically receive and record when you interact with our server, is only kept for the duration the wallet is online and Standard mode is switched off. (c) Lightning_id known as the Public key of your wallet (unique identifier of lightning node in the lightning network in hex representation), is received by the Peach server only when the wallet user interacts with one of the three additional features of the Extended mode (see paragraph 1). This data is only kept for the duration the wallet is online and Standard mode is switched off. The information above is collected with the sole purpose of allowing the Extended mode functionality to the user. By clicking the optional consent button, you allow collecting anonymized usage statistics via Google Analytics and Firebase. Please note, that we collect this data even in Standard mode but only if you have explicitly opted in for us to do so during the Wallet setup. This process does not interact with our servers and does not provide any personal data. The information we process here is fully anonymized and could not be used to track down a particular person or device. We do NOT collect, nor have access to, the following information: (a) Your private keys from cryptocurrencies; these are only accessible through your own device; and (b) information on the transactions you perform through the using of the Software. 2. If the recipient does send a payment request, the Peach server does not even participate in this exchange at all, and no information is shared. (b) For statistical research General. Subject to your consent, we use automatic tools like Google Analytics and Firebase to perform anonymized statistical research into general trends regarding the use of our services. Such anonymized information may include the following statistics: • Device type • Button clicks • Screen openings • Session duration • Geographic location Interests and benefits. Statistical research helps us to develop better services and offerings, to improve the design and content of our Software. c.
